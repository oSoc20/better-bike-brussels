BetterBikeBrussels

Better Bike Brussels is a new application that will help improve cycling in Brussels.

Open the app Contribute

The project

An application imagined by Brussels citizens during #BrusselsHacksTheCrisis, created by students from Open Summer of Code 2020 and supported by Brussels Smart City and the Brussels Regional Informatics Centre.

Its number one priority is to provide data that caters to cyclists' needs. You wonder how? With an integrated map, repair services, and even an agenda with the latest cycling events, your bike rides will be well planned.

All this data will ensure easier, safer, and more careless bike rides in the city.

Better Bike Brussels is your digital guide for all your future bike rides in Brussels.

Watch the demo video to see how this application will work for you!
